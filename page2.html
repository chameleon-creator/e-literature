This is the story of my daughter, Lila.

While the world grew quiet, she dug her hands into the earth.

She tended a small garden, a patch of stubborn hope. She whispered to the seeds, begging them for color.

But the flowers that bloomed were pale, their petals the color of ash, as if they too were holding their breath.

Hover over the flowers...

Still, she did not give up.
